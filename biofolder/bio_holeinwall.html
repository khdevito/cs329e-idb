About Hole in the Wall

For 15 years Cedar Street Courtyard has been perfectly pairing two of Austin’s favorite indulgences: Nightly live music and sumptuous cocktails. Bands range from bluesy jazz groups with horns and a big band flare to celebrated local and touring artists to rockin’ 70’s and 80’s cover bands for pure party fun. For the past eight years, Cedar Street becomes a perennial SXSW hotspot, featuring some of the biggest acts in the world. Cedar Street is truly one of the reasons Austin is the live music capital of the world. The sunken courtyard provides an intimate setting for guests to enjoy music nightly. Skilled bartenders shake up Austin’s best martinis and specialty drinks while offering a wide variety of beers and wines too. Cedar Street’s Main Lounge features a great selection of cigars which can be enjoyed in the open-air courtyard. The VIP Veranda is the perfect perch to enjoy a bird’s eye view of the band and a fresh cocktail is only a few steps away.
