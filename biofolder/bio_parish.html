About Parish

Located in the heart of downtown Austin in the historic 6th Street district, Parish is widely regarded as the best indoor live music venue in Austin, offering the highest quality production for artists and private events alike. Boasting a 450 capacity, Parish provides an intimate atmosphere for fans to experience musical legends such as Pete Townshend, Slash, Perry Farrell, and Thom Yorke, as well as up and coming artists such as Alt-J, Tycho, ZZ Ward, and Bonobo.

A multi-genre venue, Parish has been influential in the success of Austin musicians such as Spoon and Shakey Graves, while hosting shows spanning the gamut of indie rock to electronic to hip hop to funk and everywhere in between. In addition to its stellar reputation for live music, Parish also welcomes the Moontower Comedy Festival, the CMA Songwriters series, various PBS Showcases, live podcast tapings, and numerous conferences and corporate events annually.

The Parish also hosts private events, ranging from corporate events to elaborate wedding receptions. Our catering, bar, A/V and entertainment booking services provide a complete package to easily plan any event.
