The Mohawk

About The Mohawk

Handmade in downtown Austin Texas, the Mohawk was built in 2006 as a one of a kind Bar & Venue. Its mission is to host friends, musicians, and staff that represent all music tastes and all walks of life. All are welcome at the Mohawk.
The Mohawk draws its inspiration from the Native American culture of the Northeast, not from the hairdo. But if you've got one, feel free to bring it on over.
Austin is known as the Live Music Capital of the World, and has one of the single greatest collections of performing and visual talents on the planet, so in the spirit of the Mohawk Nation, the Mohawk was built and is driven by a tribe of Austinites who try everyday to bring something different to the table. From poster and digital art and clever renovation to booking and blogging, the Mohawk is the home to a diverse creative culture.
