Spider House Cafe

About Spider House Cafe

One of Austin’s legendary hotspots since 1995, Spider House Patio Bar & Cafe offers a laid back environment for it’s patrons to meet friends over delicious specialty cocktails and an artisan inspired menu each day and night. Work remotely courtesy of the free Wi-Fi until 2AM every night. The inside bar offers plenty of nooks and crannies for work or pleasure. Our cafe features gourmet coffee with a wide selection of local and craft beers on tap, a seasonal wine list, full bar, as well as refreshing cocktails that feature fruity concoctions from local favorites JuiceLand.

The Outdoor Patio blanketed in Christmas lights, has earned itself national attention from travel and destination blogs. It includes one-of-a-kind antiques and art installation pieces, a novelty collection of garden statues, and an abundance of booths, swings, and table tops. The premises includes two outdoor stages, including an Outdoor Tented “Chapel” area perfect for outdoor events and film screenings which comes equipped with it’s own Tiki Bar, making it perfect for private party rentals!

Two attached FREE PARKING lots and plenty of free street parking surrounds Spider House. Notable amount of bike racks on site make us BIKE FRIENDLY!!!
