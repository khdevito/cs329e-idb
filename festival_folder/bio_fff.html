Fun Fun Fun Fest

About Fun Fun Fun Fest

Fun Fun Fun Fest ("FFF") was an annual music and comedy festival held in Austin, Texas. The festival was the only genre based festival in the United States, featuring stages that focus specifically on hip-hop / electronica, indie rock, punk / metal, and comedy. Started in 2006, the festival focused on a combination of discovering emerging talent, putting together either rarely seen or anticipated reunion performances. In 2011, Fun Fun Fun Fest was moved to Auditorium Shores, a much larger downtown park, a change from previous years in which the event was held at Waterloo Park. The festival had its final show in 2015, and now moved outside of Austin where it is called Sound on Sound Music Festival.
