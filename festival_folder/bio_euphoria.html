Euphoria Music Festival

About Euphoria

Euphoria Music Festival is an annual four-day music and camping festival held in Austin, Texas. Euphoria focuses on electronic music but includes hip hop, indie, and jam band artists. As an immersive arts festival, Euphoria features camping, art installations, live painting, yoga and workshops. The festival debuted in 2012 and will celebrate its sixth year in the spring of 2017. Over 50,000 attendees gathered at Carson Creek Ranch for Euphoria 2016.

The 55 acre venue, Carson Creek Ranch, sits on the bank of the Colorado River and is approximately five minutes from the Austin-Bergstrom International Airport by car.[1] The 2016 festival site featured three stages: Euphoria, Elements and the Dragonfly Amphitheater. The campgrounds located across Carson Creek hosted over 5,000 campers, plus another exclusive stage and silent disco.

Missing out on Euphoria? Check out Parish, Spider House Cafe, or Emo's Austin for related music.

Time of Year & Avg. Ticket Price

Euphoria takes place at the early fall each year, generally in April.

Tickets are approximately $200 for a 3 day general admission pass and $300 for a 4-day camping + general admission pass.
