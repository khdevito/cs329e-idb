South By Southwest

About SXSW

South by Southwest (SXSW) is an annual conglomerate of film, interactive media, and music festivals and conferences that take place in mid-March in Austin, Texas, United States. It began in 1987, and has continued to grow in both scope and size every year. SXSW Music is the largest music festival of its kind in the world, with more than 2,000 acts as of 2014.SXSW Music offers artist-provided music and video samples of featured artists at each festival via their official YouTube channel.
The music event has grown from 700 registrants in 1987 to over 28,000 registrants.

Time of Year & Avg. Ticket Price

SXSW takes place annually each Spring, typically in March.

General Admission to events is usually free, however, badges of varying status can be purchased for up to $1,500 for premier access to events.
