Stubbs BBQ

About Stubbs BBQ

A local favorite for great live music and even better BBQ. Stubbs BBQ is a great destination point for the local and the tourist. Originally a Lubbock restaurant in 1968, Stubbs BBQ transformed to one of the foundations of music in Texas. Musicians such as Stevie Ray Vaughn and Willie Nelson have played the venue and it continues to host concerts many nights of the week. The original Lubbock location is closed, but at 801 Red River St, one can find some amazing music and food and experience one of the greatest locations in Austin.
