Urban Music Festival

About Urban Music Fest

Urban Music Festival (often abbreviated as "AUMF") is a two-day, national and local entertainment stage hosting, family-centric event that occurs in Austin, Texas, during the Clyde Littlefield Texas Relays. The festival features stages that focus specifically on R&B, Jazz, Indie Soul, gospel / neo soul, and showcases local and unsigned artists.

The UMF proudly plays a significant role in its local community as a part of the Austin Quality of Life Initiative. The UMF is focused on breaking new boundaries each year and will continue to do so as it aims to reach the hearts and minds of Urban music lovers where ever they may be, all are welcomed to join in the fun!

Missing out on Urban Music Festival? Check out Parish, Spider House Cafe, or Emo's Austin for related music.

Time of Year & Avg. Ticket Price

ACL takes place at the end of spring each year, generally first week of April at Town Lake Auditorium Shores.

Tickets are approximately $30 for each day of the fest.
