Hole in the Wall

About Hole in the Wall

Hole in the Wall, with its iconic slogan "Cheap Music, Fast Drinks, Live Women," is a hidden gem buried on Guadalupe street across the University of Texas campus. Amidst the cultural storm in downtown Austin, Hole in the Wall has been the venue for beginner bands, established touring artists, and rock stars. Examples of success stories after the taking the stage at Hole in the Wall are Spoon, The Gourds, Shakey Graves, Timbuk 3, Bob Schneider, Fastball, Black Joe Lewis, and Gary Clark Jr. Added bonuses are the drink specials, regular weekly performances, and an arcade!

Hole in the Wall has also been a local spot for ACL Music Festival.
