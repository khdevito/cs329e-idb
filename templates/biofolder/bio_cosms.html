COSMS

About COSMS

Formed on the first day of 2013, Austin-based COSMS is composed of a Josh and a Damen. With a sound that is as familiar, yet strange, COSMS seamlessly weaves complex rhythms with ambient soundscapes and layers riffs that roll over them. They recorded their first 4 song EP at Ohm Studios in Austin, TX in February of 2013 and returned for their duo EP, Arteria, in January of 2015.

Last year, The Sword headlined at Austin Food and Wine Festival
